Contribution No. 2

Discovery of Jupiter's Moons

What Was the Discovery?

[Describe what Galileo saw when he turned his telescope toward Jupiter. What were the four moons he discovered and what did he name them?]

What Problem Was He Trying to Solve?

[What was the scientific debate at the time about whether everything orbited Earth? How did Jupiter's moons directly challenge that idea?]

How Did He Make This Discovery?

[Walk through how he observed the moons over multiple nights and tracked their movement. What did he record and how did he document it?]

What Evidence Supports It?

[How did his nightly observations over weeks prove that these were moons orbiting Jupiter and not just background stars?]

Why Does It Matter?

[Explain why finding moons around Jupiter was so revolutionary. What did it prove about the geocentric model of the universe?]

[What was wrong with the geocentric model that Galileo could see through his telescope? What observations didn't add up?]

Contribution No. 4

Laws of Motion & Falling Objects

What Was the Discovery?

[Describe Galileo's experiments with falling objects and pendulums. What did he discover about how gravity affects objects of different masses?]

[What did Aristotle say about falling objects that Galileo disagreed with? What motivated him to test it experimentally?]

How Did He Test It?

[Describe the famous Leaning Tower of Pisa experiment and his inclined plane experiments. What was his method and what did he find?]

[How did Galileo's findings on motion directly lead to Isaac Newton's laws of physics? What would Newton not have been able to do without Galileo's groundwork?]
