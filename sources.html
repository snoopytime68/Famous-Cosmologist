Source Evaluation

How I Evaluated My Sources

✅ What Makes a Source Credible

I looked for sources written by experts, universities, scientific organizations, and trusted publishers. I also checked if the information was accurate and supported by evidence.

❌ What I Avoided

I avoided random blogs, opinion websites, and sources with no listed author or evidence because they may not contain reliable information.
